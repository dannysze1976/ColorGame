Privacy Policy for Liar Dice

Last updated: 02/12/2025

Liar Dice is developed by Dannys Sze (“we”, “us”, or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile game.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email, address, or phone number.

1.2 Automatically Collected Data (Non-Personal)

Our game may collect non-personal data through third-party services to display ads and ensure the app works properly, including:

  • Device model
  • Country/region
  • App usage statistics
  • Advertising ID (used for showing ads)

No data is used to personally identify you.

2. Third-Party Services

AdMob (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to show advertisements inside the game.

AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • App interaction data
  • Approximate location (for ad relevance)

You can learn more here: Google Privacy Policy

3. How We Use the Information

Any non-personal data collected is used for:

  • Displaying ads
  • Improving the game
  • Ensuring performance and stability

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data because we do not collect any.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our game is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will remove it immediately.

5. Data Security

We do not store any personal information on our servers. Any data collected by AdMob is handled according to Google’s security standards.

6. Your Privacy Choices

You can manage ads or reset your advertising ID:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset Advertising ID
  • Settings → Google → Ads → Opt-out of Ads Personalization

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted here, and you should check this page periodically.
