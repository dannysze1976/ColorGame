About Us

Welcome to 3Sedges — where clever code meets unapologetic fun.

We are a team of innovators, designers, and DSP experts on a mission to transform the way people experience games.

Whether it’s a mobile game or a late-night party game, our creations are crafted to spark joy, competition, and connection.

At 3Sedges, we fuse smart technology with bold creativity. Our roots in Digital Signal Processing (DSP) give us the edge to deliver responsive, high-performance gameplay, while our obsession with fun keeps us focused on player experience.

Why the name? 3Sedges stands for "Free, Sharp, and on the Edge." "3" captures the spirit of freedom and play. "Sedges" hints at the cutting edge — unpredictable, bold, and never dull.

So whether you're tapping your screen or raising your glass, you’re playing with purpose — you're playing with 3Sedges.
