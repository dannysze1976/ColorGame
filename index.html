Our Product

Color Game is a digital version of a traditional Filipino drinking game — now supercharged for your phone.

Play solo, with friends, or strangers online. It's fair, vibrant, and fast.

🎲 How to Play

Color Game has two roles: Player and Banker.

🧑‍🎮 Player Mode

🎯 Choose one or more colours to bet on.
💰 Place your bet and wait for the roll.

🎲 Banker Mode

🧊 Roll 3 dice, each with 6 colour faces.
📦 Dice are rolled from a box for a random result.

🏆 Winning & Payouts

  • 🎉 1 die – win 1× your bet
  • 🎉🎉 2 dice – win 2× your bet
  • 🎉🎉🎉 3 dice – win 3× your bet

Simple, fun, fast. Great for drinks, dares, or bragging rights.
