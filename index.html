About Us

3Sedges was founded by a team of Digital Signal Processing (DSP) experts committed to pushing the boundaries of technology. We specialize in developing efficient, scalable, and intelligent DSP solutions for real-world applications. Our focus is on pioneering innovations in Quantum Computing and Edge Computing, with particular emphasis on transforming the gaming industry.

In the realm of Quantum Computing, we are exploring the power of quantum algorithms to tackle the complex and computationally intensive challenges faced by modern game development.

At the same time, Edge Computing is reshaping how games are delivered and experienced.

At 3Sedges, we integrate both quantum and edge computing into our DSP solutions for gaming, empowering game developers to push the limits of what’s possible.

Our name reflects our values: "3" symbolizes freedom—freedom to innovate. "Sedges" refers to the cutting-edge. Together, 3Sedges means open, boundaryless exploration of future technologies.

Solutions

Our DSP-based solutions integrate quantum and edge computing for real-time, high-efficiency processing across industries, especially gaming.

Our Product

Color Game is a digital adaptation of a traditional Filipino drinking game that has brought fun and excitement to countless social gatherings. In the Philippines, Color Game is a staple at festivals and parties, known for its simple rules and lively atmosphere.

We've modernized this cultural favorite with smart visuals, responsive gameplay, and integrated multiplayer support — bringing the festive thrill of the streets to your mobile device.

Whether you're playing with friends or challenging strangers online, Color Game by 3Sedges offers a fresh spin on a timeless classic, with fair odds, vibrant design, and high-performance DSP at its core.
