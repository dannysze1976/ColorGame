About Us

Welcome to 3Sedges — where clever code meets unapologetic fun.

We are a team of innovators, designers, and DSP experts on a mission to transform the way people experience games. Whether it’s through a laugh-out-loud mobile game on your phone or a late-night board game session with friends over drinks, our creations are crafted to spark joy, competition, and connection.

At 3Sedges, we fuse smart technology with bold creativity. Our roots in Digital Signal Processing (DSP) give us the edge to deliver responsive, high-performance gameplay, while our obsession with fun keeps us grounded in what matters most — the player experience.

From mobile mini-games that deliver maximum excitement to drinking games that ignite the party, our portfolio celebrates simplicity, spontaneity, and a little bit of chaos.

Why the name? 3Sedges stands for "Free, Sharp, and on the Edge." "3" captures the spirit of freedom and play. "Sedges" hints at the cutting edge — unpredictable, bold, and never dull. Together, they define our commitment to boundary-breaking fun in every form.

So whether you're tapping your screen or raising your glass, you’re playing with purpose — you're playing with 3Sedges.

Our Product

Color Game is a digital adaptation of a traditional Filipino drinking game that has brought fun and excitement to countless social gatherings. In the Philippines, Color Game is a staple at festivals and parties, known for its simple rules and lively atmosphere.

We've modernized this cultural favorite with smart visuals, responsive gameplay, and integrated multiplayer support — bringing the festive thrill of the streets to your mobile device.

Whether you're playing with friends or challenging strangers online, Color Game by 3Sedges offers a fresh spin on a timeless classic, with fair odds, vibrant design, and high-performance DSP at its core.
